Required Documents

Documents Needed For Admission

To ensure a smooth and hassle-free admission process, please keep the following documents ready while submitting your application.

Birth Certificate

Official proof of your child's date of birth.

Parent ID Proof

A valid government-issued identification document.

Address Proof

Current residential address verification document.

Medical Records

Health information and allergy details, if applicable.

Vaccination Record

Latest immunization records for your child.

Passport Photos

Recent passport-size photographs of the child.
